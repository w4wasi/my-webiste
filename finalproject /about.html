About Me

WELCOME TO ASPEN CREATIONS

Aspen Creations LLC was established in 1997 inorder to serve the theming requirements of U.A.E. and the neighboring countries. The company is 100% national private company established and managed by Mr. Hashem Al Marzouqi, U.A.E. national graduated from U.S.A. as a Bachelor of Science in Engineering (B.Sc. M.E.)

Aspen Creations LLC is today recognized as a premier theming company in the region with projects network in U.A.E., Qatar, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Morocco and UK.

Aspen Creations LLC handles turnkey projects from concept to completion, providing master planning to project management and execution of themed projects. We provide a total solution to the leisure industry and private clients, spread across the region.
